about.slowhell.xyz

slowhell.xyz is my transmission point, a sanctuary of decay, code, and slow rebirth. It started as a whisper in a dark loop: a place to document thoughts I couldn't say aloud, to code truths I didn't yet understand.

I created slowhell.xyz as a space to build deliberately. A place where code and cognition, entropy and emotion, could sit in the same breath.

I'm building software that can hold grief without crashing. Interfaces that can listen without speaking. Pages that become mirrors.

I'm a student of Computer Science, a father, and a seeker drawn to the liminal spaces between software and self. I explore the intersection of AI, nueroscience, philosophy, and the art of designing rituals through interface. My name is Tyler, but here I go by @whoami.null.xyz

slowhell.xyz isn't a product. It's a public notebook, a working archive of in-progress systems. You'll find entropy-based APIs, emotional journaling tools, and metaphysical experiments with terminal-style REPLs. I treat technology as a mirror. I code to reflect, not just execute.
